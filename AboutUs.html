üè´ About Prakasam Engineering College, Kandukur

Established in 2001 under the aegis of Amma Education Society, Prakasam Engineering College (PEC), Kandukur has emerged as a beacon of technical education in Andhra Pradesh. Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK) and approved by AICTE, the college is committed to nurturing future-ready engineers and professionals with strong ethical values.

Spread across a serene and eco-friendly campus, PEC offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs in various disciplines including Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering. The institution is equipped with modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, a vast digital library, and separate hostels for boys and girls.

Our dedicated faculty, dynamic training & placement cell, and industry-oriented curriculum empower students with the skills needed for global careers. Over the years, PEC has successfully placed thousands of graduates in top-tier companies like Infosys, Wipro, IBM, and Tech Mahindra.

At PEC, we believe in holistic development, blending academics with innovation, research, sports, and social responsibility. Our mission is to shape competent engineers who not only excel in their fields but also contribute positively to society.
